- 2019

chip is a side table intended to be a warm, human accent. the arrangement of legs creates enough space underneath for a speaker, small pet or potted plant. the leg joints are precisely machined so that the piece assembles without glue, and can be disassembled, moved, and reassembled easily.

i wanted to create a piece that looked and felt handmade, with the degree of craftsmanship expected in a manufactured piece. i chose to work with ash because of its prominent grain and low price point. at the time of chip's creation i was a student and wanted the ability to move the piece without hassle. using a friction fit accomplished these goals and eliminated the need for glue or hardware, making the piece more environmentally friendly.
