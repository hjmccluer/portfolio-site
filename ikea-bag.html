- 2020

The ikea bag came together over the course of an afternoon. I needed the storage space of an ikea tote and the mobility of a backpack. The goal was a cheap, sturdy bag for carrying groceries in.

After cutting the ikea tote along its seams, I realized it could be easily resewn into a simple drawstring backpack with little modification or extra material needed. I pulled the lining material, strap terminals and drawstring from another bag project I had laying around.
