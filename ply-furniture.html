- 2021

Ply is a series of furniture created in response to the issues of fast-fashion and wasteful production in the modern furniture industry. Each piece is constructed solely of sheet material and functions without any glue or fasteners. Their planar components can be cut on one CNC and flat packed, saving warehousing and shipping space.

Because the pieces themselves are not designed to be objects of decoration or status, the emphasis naturally falls on how the user interacts with and customizes them with personal artifacts and belongings.

This project was initiated when i moved into my first apartment and wanted to furnish it without breaking the bank. In conducting user research for the project I found that traditional options in this situation are consignment stores, facebook marketplace/craigslist, or retailers like Amazon, Walmart, IKEA, etc.

Further research revealed the environmental and ethical issues associated with the retail furniture industry. That data can be found here.
