- 2020

Voyager is a pack concept for the weekend adventurer, combining the utility of a duffel, the functionality of a backpack and the protection of a diver's wet/dry bag. Constructed of ripstop nylon, its features include: laptop sleeve, articulated padding, removable liner, and a quick-access document pocket/camera case.

This project was initiated during a soft goods design course. After the course I continued refining the bag from its felt prototype into the ripstop nylon iteration seen above. I am currently working on sewing a Cordura model for durability testing.
